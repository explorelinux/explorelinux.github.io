Follow the below mentioned steps to enable workspaces in Linux.

  1. Go to the top-right corner of the desktop and click on the username(In this case it is Saketh).Then select "System settings".
  2. A window appears.In that select "Appearance".
  3. A window appears.In that select "Behaviour"(see the screenshot below).
  4. Now click on the checkbox "Enable workspaces".
  5. Now close the window.On the launcher(The left most column on the desktop),click on the bottom most icon(see the screenshot)
  6. Four individual workspaces can be seen.
  7. To migrate from one workspace to another workspace,press "ctr + alt + arrow key" or click on the wokspace icon(Bottom most)on launcher(Left most column) and proceed.
