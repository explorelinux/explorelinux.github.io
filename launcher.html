How to remove icons from launcher?

  1. Right click on an icon you want to remove from launcher.
  2. Select "Unlock from launcher."
  3. However some icons such as "search icon","Workspace switcher","Trash" can't be removed from launcher.

How to insert an icon on launcher?

  1. Open an application whose icon is to be locked to launcher.
  2. Now right click on an icon(of required application)which is present on launcher.
  3. Select "Lock to Launcher" option.
  4. Your icon is now locked to the launcher.
